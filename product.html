The everyday tablecloth, reimagined.

Our cotton linen tablecloth is woven from 100% natural fibers on traditional looms in Shandong, China — a region with centuries of textile craft heritage. The weave is tight enough for durability but loose enough to breathe, giving it that characteristic linen texture that softens beautifully with each wash.

The hand-knotted tassels along both ends are made by the same artisans who weave the fabric. No two are exactly alike — and that's the point. Each tablecloth carries the subtle irregularities of handwork, the marks that distinguish craft from mass production.

Use it for everyday meals or special gatherings. It works equally well over a bare wooden table or layered over a solid table cover. The more you wash it, the softer it becomes.

Materials

  • Composition: 55% Cotton, 45% Linen
  • Weight: 180 g/m² (medium weight)
  • Origin: Woven in Shandong, China
  • Certification: Oeko-Tex Standard 100, REACH compliant
  • Dye: Reactive dye, low-impact, AZO-free

Care Instructions

  • Machine wash at 30°C on gentle cycle
  • Do not bleach. Use mild detergent only.
  • Tumble dry low or line dry. Linen wrinkles naturally — embrace it or iron on medium heat while damp.
  • Gets softer with each wash. Pre-shrunk during manufacturing.
  • Avoid fabric softeners — they coat natural fibers and reduce absorbency.
