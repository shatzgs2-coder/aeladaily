Our Story

We believe the objects we live with should be honest, natural, and made to last.

AELADAILY was born from a simple conviction: that nourishment isn't a luxury reserved for special occasions. It's a daily practice.

We draw from Wabi-Sabi — the Japanese aesthetic that finds beauty in imperfection, simplicity, and the natural aging of materials. A cotton linen tablecloth that softens with each wash. A hand-woven rope basket that tells the story of its maker.
